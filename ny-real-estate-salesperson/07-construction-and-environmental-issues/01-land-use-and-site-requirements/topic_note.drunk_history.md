A building permit is an official approval issued by a local government agency that allows construction, major renovations, or utility installations to proceed on a property. Why does the government care? Because they are terrified of your client! Building permits ensure that proposed construction work complies with local building codes, zoning laws, and safety regulations so the whole thing doesn’t collapse onto the sidewalk.

The classic method for this subsurface magic is the conventional absorption trench, but the earth has to be willing to play ball. A conventional subsurface absorption trench system requires a minimum of four feet of usable soil existing above bedrock and groundwater. It needs breathing room.
