But what about the early stages of training, when a mistake could blow the whole operation? Errorless learning is an instructional sequence designed to prevent students from making mistakes during the initial acquisition phase of a new skill. As they get better, you have to back off to prevent prompt dependency. Time delay is an instructional sequencing strategy that introduces a brief pause between a discriminative stimulus and a prompt to encourage independent responding.
