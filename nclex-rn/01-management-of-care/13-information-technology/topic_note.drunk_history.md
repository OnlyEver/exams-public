Now, let’s talk about the digital vault. The Electronic Health Record is the most heavily guarded ledger on earth, protected by massive federal watchdogs. The Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act mandates the protection of electronic protected health information. That’s the original omertà—the blood oath of privacy. But as the internet got scarier and more complex, the feds doubled down. The Health Information Technology for Economic and Clinical Health Act expands privacy and security provisions for electronic health records, bringing down a massive hammer on anyone caught leaking secrets.

Facilities track electronic health record access through unique user login audit trails. These glowing digital footprints never wash away, and audit trails identify unauthorized viewing of protected health information by staff members. This means you stay strictly in your lane!
